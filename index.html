About Me

Hello! My name is Anna, currently a senior B.S. Computer Science candidate at Case Western Reserve University graduating in the spring of 2017. I wasn't familiar with what Computer Science was or what it was capable of doing prior to college. Coming to college and taking my first programming course was eye opening, as I was able to build something cool from my very first programming class. Though I can't touch and mold software like clay, I revel in the feeling of building things using the latest technology out of seemingly nothing.

I have worked as a full stack software engineer but have discovered that my interest lies in the backend. I also love attending architectural meetings, to brainstorm what technologies to use to solve what kind of problems. I'm always able to learn something new, and the more I learn, the more I realize there is so much more to learn. A Chinese proverb I embrace a lot says "Three people walk together, there must be a teacher of mine among them." I've always been able to learn things from classmates, co-workers, and even the students that I teach to and firmly believe that anyone and everyone can become a teacher of mine.
